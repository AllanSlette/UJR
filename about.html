About us

Urban Junk Removal is a local, family, full-service junk removal and trash hauling company. We will pick up and haul off any unwanted items around your home or office. No job is too big or too small for us. We ensure the best customer service every time. Call Urban Junk Removal today and let us show you why we are the gold standard in junk removal in Northwest Arkansas.

Family Owned & Operated

Family comes first with us. As a family-owned and operated business, we value your business and promise to show respect, integrity, and trust to each of our customers.

Transparent Pricing

We pride ourselves on our transparent pricing. Have any questions on how we price our services or need an estimate? Contact us today.

Respectful Service

Your time and property are our concern and will be treated with the most respect. We work hard to make sure our customers receive the best service each time.

Environmentally Friendly

When it comes to junk removal we strive to take all the necessary steps to make sure all possible items are recycled or donated.
